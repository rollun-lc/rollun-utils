# Privacy Policy and Experimental Feature Disclaimer

Last updated: [2026-05-07]

## 1. Overview

This GPT-based assistant is an experimental internal feature created to help users interact with selected work-management and time-tracking tools, including Trello and Clockify.

The feature is provided for testing, evaluation, and internal workflow assistance only. It is not a production-grade system, not an official reporting system, and not a source of legally or operationally binding conclusions.

By using this assistant, you acknowledge that its outputs may be incomplete, inaccurate, outdated, or based on limited external data returned by connected services.

## 2. Experimental Status and No Warranty

This assistant is provided on an “as is” and “as available” basis.

We do not guarantee that the assistant will:

- always return complete or accurate information;
- correctly interpret Trello cards, Clockify time entries, comments, labels, checklists, or attachments;
- correctly calculate productivity, workload, remaining effort, or work progress;
- always access external services successfully;
- be available without interruption;
- be free from errors, omissions, delays, or incorrect assumptions.

All outputs generated by the assistant must be independently reviewed before being used for management decisions, reporting, performance evaluation, planning, billing, disciplinary action, or any other important business decision.

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, we disclaim liability for decisions made solely or primarily based on the assistant’s output.

## 3. Purpose of Processing

The assistant may process data only for the following purposes:

- reading and summarizing existing Trello cards;
- helping create or improve ticket wording;
- reading Trello card metadata, comments, checklists, labels, members, and attachments where available;
- looking up Clockify users;
- reading Clockify time entries;
- calculating or summarizing tracked time based on returned Clockify data;
- assisting with internal workflow analysis;
- testing and evaluating the usefulness of the assistant.

The assistant should not be used as the only source of truth for employee productivity, performance, payroll, legal compliance, or contractual reporting.

## 4. Data That May Be Processed

Depending on the user request and connected tools, the assistant may process the following categories of data:

- user names;
- email addresses or account identifiers;
- Trello card titles, descriptions, comments, labels, checklists, members, due dates, attachments, and activity data;
- Clockify workspace data;
- Clockify user data;
- Clockify time entries, including descriptions, projects, tasks, dates, durations, and related metadata;
- user-provided prompts and clarifications;
- technical identifiers required to access connected services, such as Trello user identifiers or proxy tokens.

The exact data processed depends on the request made by the user and the data returned by Trello, Clockify, or related internal proxy services.

## 5. Connected Third-Party and Internal Services

This assistant may interact with external or internal services, including:

- Trello;
- Clockify;
- internal proxy/API services used to authorize or route requests.

When the assistant accesses these services, data may be transmitted to or received from those services according to their own technical behavior, API responses, and applicable privacy terms.

Users should not submit confidential, sensitive, or unnecessary personal data unless it is required for the intended workflow.

## 6. Legal Basis

Where applicable, personal data may be processed on one or more of the following legal bases:

- legitimate interest in testing and improving internal workflow tools;
- legitimate interest in managing internal work processes;
- performance of employment-related or contractor-related administrative processes;
- user consent, where consent is specifically requested;
- compliance with applicable legal obligations, where relevant.

The applicable legal basis may depend on the organization, user role, jurisdiction, and specific use case.

## 7. Human Review Required

The assistant may generate summaries, estimates, productivity interpretations, ticket drafts, and recommendations.

However:

- the assistant does not replace human judgment;
- the assistant may misunderstand context;
- the assistant may miss data not available through connected APIs;
- Clockify data alone may not fully represent actual work;
- Trello activity alone may not fully represent investigation, debugging, communication, blockers, or context switching;
- productivity-related conclusions must be reviewed by a responsible human before use.

No automated decision with significant effect on a person should be made solely based on the assistant’s output.

## 8. Accuracy and Data Limitations

The assistant can only work with information that is available to it at the time of the request.

The assistant may produce incorrect or incomplete results if:

- Trello cards are incomplete;
- Clockify time entries are missing or inaccurate;
- users tracked time under different descriptions;
- API responses are incomplete;
- access permissions are limited;
- task context exists outside Trello or Clockify;
- the user request is ambiguous;
- the assistant makes an incorrect interpretation.

Users are responsible for verifying important outputs against the original source systems.

## 9. Data Retention

This policy does not define a fixed retention period for data stored by Trello, Clockify, internal proxy services, or the GPT platform.

Data retention may depend on:

- the retention settings of Trello;
- the retention settings of Clockify;
- internal company retention rules;
- platform-level logging or conversation history settings;
- legal, accounting, security, or operational requirements.

Where possible, personal data should be retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

## 10. Access Control

Access to this assistant should be limited to authorized users.

Users must not:

- share access links with unauthorized persons;
- use another person’s credentials or identifiers without permission;
- request data they are not authorized to access;
- use the assistant to bypass Trello, Clockify, company, or platform access controls.

The organization may revoke access to the assistant at any time.

## 11. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures should be used to protect data processed through this assistant.

However, because the assistant is experimental, users should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive information, including:

- government identification numbers;
- financial account details;
- medical or health information;
- private personal messages;
- passwords, API keys, or secrets;
- confidential information unrelated to the work task.

If sensitive information is accidentally submitted, users should report it to the responsible internal contact.
